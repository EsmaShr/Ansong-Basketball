ANSONG BASKETBALL

MICHAEL ANSONG
LEAD SKILLS TRAINER

Before becoming a skills trainer, Mike attended Bordentown Regional HS, where he played all four years. In his sophomore year was named offensive player of the year award because of his proficiency and skill level to create for himself and his teammates. He continued his career at Bordentown Regional HS as a prolific scorer. He also went on to play collegiate ball and have a taste for every division level, he did a little bit of everything over his coaching/training experience.
He has coached, the Central Jersey Jammers, a travel basketball organization located in New Brunswick, NJ for about 3 years.

This was a great learning experience for him dealing with all age groups and being able to commence small group clinics for player development was a plus, especially for players preparing themselves for the upcoming season. The highest level of coaching for him is on the Amateur athletic union level. He worked with his local gym as a private skills trainer for basketball and trained anyone that was a member/nonmember.
