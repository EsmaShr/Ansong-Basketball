Training Services

Take a look at our Training options:

Private (1-on-1) Sessions

Training Session with Coach Mike in a private 1-on-1 setting. All private sessions are a one-hour workout.
Ansong Basketball strategically designs each drill so that the athlete's weaknesses become their strengths. These workouts are booked on a first-come-first-serve basis. To book your 1-on-1 private training session, click Private 1-on-1.

Semi-Private (2-6 players) Sessions

We offer middle school and high school semi-private groups. These workouts are booked on a first-come-first-serve basis.
Training by yourself can help improve your basketball ability but training with a partner can initiate the competitive aspect between players. To book your Semi-Private training session, click Semi-Private.

Team Training Sessions

We offer team training, our specialist doesn't just work out players. We solve problems and offer solutions. Teams will have the chance to train together in a focused environment. To book your Team training session, click Team training.

NEW

Online Training Session

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we are offering exclusive virtual training via Zoom. We've established weekly sessions to keep players engaged in sport activities.
