Training Services

Take a look at our Training options:

Private (1-on-1) Sessions

Training Session with Coach Mike in a private 1-on-1 setting. All private sessions are a one-hour workout.
Ansong Basketball strategically designs each drill so that the athlete's weaknesses become their strengths. These workouts are booked on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Semi-Private (2-6 players) Sessions

We offer middle school and high school semi-private groups. These workouts are booked on a first-come-first-serve basis.
Training by yourself can help improve your basketball ability but training with a partner can initiate the competitive aspect between players.

Team Training Sessions

We offer team training, our specialist doesn't just work out players.
We solve problems and offer solutions. Teams will have the chance to train together in a focused environment.
